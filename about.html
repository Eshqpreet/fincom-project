The FinCOM Club of IIT Ropar gives the students rigorous training in the domains of Finance, Consulting, Analytics, Stock market, product management, etc. Students consistently engage in live projects, case study competitions, and design thinking competitions. It has succeeded in creating an environment conducive to learning and skill development. The club runs its flagship courses. Data101 was completed successfully and had more than 600 enrollments; currently running, PM102 (course on Product Management) has around 350 enrollments. It conducts regular seminars, assignments, panel discussions by guest speakers, and various interactive sessions with VC/PEs, Investment bankers, PMs, and different professionals.
